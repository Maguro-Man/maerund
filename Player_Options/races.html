Races

Human
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +1 bonus to three different ability scores of your choice. Humans are versatile and individuals can vary greatly from each other.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature. The average height and build of a Human varies between regions, but they are typically between five and six feet tall and weigh between 100 and 160 pounds.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common as well as one of the following languages: Vrillian, Serelic, or Tyrili.
  • Perseverance: When you roll a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die. You must take the new result. Grim prospects only serve as further encouragement for Humans to overcome their obstacles.
  • Versatility: You gain proficiency in two skills of your choice. Human cultures can be quite different, and so are the skills they may learn growing up.

Eldaari
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Intelligence and a +1 bonus to Dexterity. The Eldaari are known for their scholarly achievements, and their slender frames make them naturally agile.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature. Few Eldaari are shorter than six feet tall; the average Eldaari height is six feet four inches. They are also relatively thin, with an average weight of 140 pounds for an Eldaari of average height.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Eldaarish.
  • Fundamental Magic: You know one cantrip of your choice from the wizard spell list. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it. All Eldaari, even if they do not study it further, are introduced to magic at a young age.
  • Arcane Insight: You can cast Detect Thoughts once per day. When you reach 3rd level, you can also cast Detect Magic once per day. When you reach 5th level, you can also cast See Invisbility spell once per day. You must finish a long rest in order to cast any of these spells again using this trait. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for these spells. Eldaari perception is enhanced by their innate affinity for magic.
  • Arcane Poise: You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep. Generations of exposure to magic have made Eldaari minds less vulnerable to magical manipulation.

Feldani
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Dexterity and a +1 bonus to Constitution.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 35 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Feldan.
  • Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
  • Guerilla Fighter: If you surprise a creature and hit it with an attack on your first turn in combat, the attack deals an extra 2d6 damage to it. You can use this trait only once per combat.
  • Blades in the Grass: You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena.

Dwerger
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Constitution and a +1 bonus to Wisdom.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 25 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Runic.
  • Superior Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 120 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
  • Short Stature: You are count as one size smaller when squeezing into a smaller space.
  • Stable Stance: You count as one size larger when determining if another creature is able to grapple or shove you.
  • Hardy Physique: Your hit point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 every time you gain a level.
  • Heart of the Dwerger: You have resistance against cold damage.
