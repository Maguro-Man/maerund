Eldaari

Our empire may be lost, but soon it will be but a footnote in the history of our great people. Those who live for today will only lose sight of tomorrow. We Eldaari look to the future; our patience and foresight is our strength. What belongs to us will be returned, it is inevitable, even if those who took it from us are already long gone and we must fill it with the bodies of their grandchildren. The sins of the father will be repaid in full.

Queen Amaryll, speech given before the Eldaari Sundering

Racial Traits

  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Intelligence and a +1 bonus to Dexterity.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Eldaarish.
  • Cantrip: You know one cantrip of your choice from the wizard spell list. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it.
  • Arcane Insight: You can cast Detect Thoughts once per day. When you reach 3rd level, you can also cast Detect Magic once per day. When you reach 5th level, you can also cast See Invisbility spell once per day. You must finish a long rest in order to cast any of these spells again using this trait. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for these spells.
  • Arcane Poise: You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep.
