Racial Traits

  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Strength and a +1 bonus to Wisdom. Magians are well-known for their immense strength as well as their patient demeanor.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature. Magnians are not particularly short or tall, with an average height of 5 feet 6 inches, but are extremely heavy, weighing on average upwards of 250 pounds.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Magnar.
  • Powerful Build: You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift. You also count as one size larger when determining if you are able to grapple or shove another creature. The sheer weight and strength of Magnians is extraordinary for their size.
  • Arm Slam: You are proficient with your unarmed strikes, which deal 1d6 bludgeoning damage on a hit. Magnians can swing their arms like cudgels, inflicting much more damage than a typical untrained punch.
  • Long-Limbed: When you make a melee attack on your turn, your reach for it is 5 feet greater than normal. Magnians have especially long arms that allow them to lunge further when attacking.
  • Tackle: When you take the Dash action and move at least 20 feet, you may take a Shove action at the end of your movement. A charging Magnian can use their own mass a weapon.
