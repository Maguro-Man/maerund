House Rules

Feats

  • Lucky: You have 3 luck points. Whenever you make an attack roll, an ability check, or a saving throw, you can spend one luck point to roll a d6 and add the number rolled to the total result. You can choose to spend one of your luck points after you roll the die, but before the outcome is determined. You can also spend one luck point when an attack roll is made against you. Roll a d6 and subtract the number rolled from the total result. This die becomes a d8 at 5th level, a d10 at 10th level, and a d12 at 15th level. You regain your expended luck points when you finish a long rest.
