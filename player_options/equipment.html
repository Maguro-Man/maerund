Armor

Equipment names and descriptions have been reworked for flavor and setting purposes. There have been no mechanical changes to the prices or attributes of armor. Weapons have been removed and reworked to ensure different choices are meaningful: for example, the “morningstar” and “trident” weapons have been removed for being heavier and more expensive versions of otherwise identical alternatives. Removed equipment, and any other equipment that was not already present in the original equipment list, can still exist within the setting as reskins of appropriately similar listed equipment.
