Races

Human
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +1 bonus to three different ability scores of your choice. Humans are versatile and individuals can vary greatly from each other.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature. The average height and build of a Human varies between regions, but they are typically between five and six feet tall and weigh between 100 and 160 pounds.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common as well as one of the following languages: Vrillian, Serelic, or Tyrili.
  • Perseverance: When you roll a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die. You must take the new result. Grim prospects only serve as further encouragement for Humans to overcome their obstacles.
  • Versatility: You gain proficiency in two skills of your choice. Human cultures can be quite different, and so are the skills they may learn growing up.

Eldaari
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Intelligence and a +1 bonus to Dexterity. The Eldaari are known for their scholarly achievements, and their slender frames make them naturally agile.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature. Few Eldaari are shorter than six feet tall; the average Eldaari height is six feet four inches. They are also relatively thin, with an average weight of 140 pounds for an Eldaari of average height.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Eldaarish.
  • Fundamental Magic: You know one cantrip of your choice from the Wizard spell list. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it. All Eldaari, even if they do not study it further, are introduced to magic at a young age.
  • Arcane Insight: You can cast Detect Thoughts once per day. When you reach 3rd level, you can also cast Detect Magic once per day. When you reach 5th level, you can also cast See Invisbility spell once per day. You must finish a long rest in order to cast any of these spells again using this trait. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for these spells. Eldaari perception is enhanced by their innate affinity for magic.
  • Arcane Poise: You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put you to sleep. Generations of exposure to magic have made Eldaari minds less vulnerable to magical manipulation.

Feldani
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Dexterity and a +1 bonus to Constitution.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 35 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Feldan.
  • Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
  • Guerilla Fighter: If you surprise a creature and hit it with an attack on your first turn in combat, the attack deals an extra 2d6 damage to it. You can use this trait only once per combat.
  • Blades in the Grass: You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena.

Dwerger
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Constitution and a +1 bonus to Wisdom.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 25 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Runic.
  • Superior Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 120 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
  • Short Stature: You are count as one size smaller when squeezing into a smaller space.
  • Stable Stance: You count as one size larger when determining if another creature is able to grapple or shove you.
  • Hardy Physique: Your hit point maximum increases by 1, and it increases by 1 every time you gain a level.
  • Heart of the Dwerger: You have resistance against cold damage.

Ul'Raathi
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Strength and a +1 bonus to Constitution.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common, Ul'Raathan, and one extra language of your choice. The Ul'Raathi are a traditionally seafaring nation; Ul'Raathi merchants and mercenaries alike learn the languages of their clients in order to ply their trade more effectively.
  • Unyielding: When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright; you can drop to 1 hit point instead. You can't use this feature again until you finish a long rest.
  • Fearless: You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
  • Perfect Blow: When you score a critical hit with a melee weapon attack, you can roll one of the weapon's damage dice one additional time and add it to the extra damage of the critical hit.
  • Tireless Advance: As a bonus action, you can move up to your movement speed toward a hostile creature you can see or hear. You must end this move closer to the enemy than you started.

Saurian
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Dexterity and a +1 bonus to Wisdom.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed and a 30 foot swim speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Wyverian.
  • Darkvision: You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
  • Hold Breath: You can hold your breath for up to 15 minutes at a time.
  • Natural Armor: When you aren't wearing armor, your AC is 13 + your Dexterity modifier. You can use your natural armor to determine your AC if the armor you wear would leave you with a lower AC. A shield's benefits apply as normal while you use your natural armor.
  • Claws: Your claws are natural weapons, which you can use to make unarmed strikes. If you hit with them, you deal slashing damage equal to 1d6 + your Strength modifier, instead of bludgeoning damage normal for an unarmed strike.
  • Wetland Camouflage: You have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks to hide in water or muddy terrain.

Magnian
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Strength and a +1 bonus to Wisdom.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Magnar.
  • Powerful Build: You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift. You also count as one size larger when determining if you are able to grapple or shove another creature.
  • Arm Slam: You are proficient with your unarmed strikes, which deal 1d6 bludgeoning damage on a hit.
  • Long-Limbed: When you make a melee attack on your turn, your reach for it is 5 feet greater than normal.
  • Tackle: When you take the Dash action and move at least 20 feet, you may take a Shove action at the end of your movement.

Tatsu
  • Ability Scores: You gain a +2 bonus to Charisma and a +1 bonus to Wisdom.
  • Size: You are a Medium creature.
  • Movement: You have a 30 foot land speed.
  • Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Daitien.
  • Dragon Breath: You can use your action to exhale destructive energy. Your Imperial House determines the damage type of the exhalation. When you use your breath weapon, each creature in a 15 foot cone must make a saving throw, the type of which is determined by your breath type. The DC for this saving throw equals 8 + your Constitution modifier + your proficiency bonus. A creature takes 2d6 damage on a failed save, and half as much damage on a successful one. The damage increases to 3d6 at 6th level, 4d6 at 11th level, and 5d6 at 16th level. After you use your breath weapon, you can't use it again until you complete a short or long rest.
    • Imperial House Damage Type
    Azure Lightning (Dex Save)
    Vermillion Fire (Dex Save)
    Platinum Cold (Con Save)
    Onyx Poison (Con Save)
  • Draconic Resistance: You have resistance to the damage type associated with your Imperial House.
  • Imperial Tongue: When your reach 3rd level, you can cast the Charm Person spell as a 2nd-level spell once with this trait. When you reach 5th level, you can cast the Enthrall spell once with this trait. You must finish a long rest to cast these spells once again with this trait. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells.
